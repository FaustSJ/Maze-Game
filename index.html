Polite Parasite!

  • Use the arrow keys to move around!
  • High Concept: Navigate the maze while avoiding white blood cells.
  • Player Experience Goals: I want the player to feel frustration or determination.
  • Genre: Arcade puzzle game!
  • Game play: Use the arrow keys to move around the maze. Avoid the white blood cells.
  • Prototype goal: I'm trying to figure out how to make tile games using the Tiled program.
  • Features: A maze of the human anatomy!
  • Setting/Premise: You are a parasite inside of a human body.
  • Story: You are a parasite that craves the innerds of reptiles. Unfortunately, the one that consummed you is not a reptile. You have no interest in harming this human, though the human's defense system doesn't agree. Starting from the asophogus, you must safely navigate to the pelvis to be free of your human victum.
  • Target Audience: People who like puzzle games.
  • Play time: Perhaps two or three minutes.
  • Strategies: The standard maze strategies apply here. If you hit a dead end, go back to your last cross-path and take another path.
  • Prototype Assets: background:http://www.thaidentist.org/human-body-anatomy-model/human-body-anatomy-model/ PlayerSprite and Portal: from phaser examples
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: Any other maze game.
