Doki Doki Kittytown

  • Use the mouse to pick your actions!
  • High Concept: Try and win the heart of the kitty!
  • Player Experience Goals: I want player to crave the kitty's love!
  • Genre: This game is a Dating Sim.
  • Game play: Simply click which actions you wish to do or which phrases you wish to say.
  • Prototype goal: Figuring out how to make dating sim type games! :D
  • Features: A dating sim! No action or enemies! Enjoy the hormones.
  • Setting/Premise: You are the new student at a kigh school and you want to find love.
  • Story: You are a new student at school. Your objective is to find love and it looks like the girl next to you is interested.
  • Target Audience: People into romance visual novel games.
  • Play time: Less then a minute.
  • Strategies: Choose what you say and do carefully! Kitty will let you know if you're doing good or not by her reactions.
